About Us

We want to build a world where businesses can reach out to the customers that want to hear from them, and where customers don't have to sift through the information they don't care about. In our connected world, someone is constantly trying to get our attention, and we're tired of that.

A spectrum is something that can be classified on a scale, and we think that fits just about everyone.With Spectrum you're able to pick what interests you, and we promise that is all you will see.

Spectrum is here for the people who want to focus on their life. We want you to spend your time living, not sifting through notifications.

Your life, your choices: When you first open our app you will be able to refine your interests. Be as detailed as you would like. As you interact with the choices presented to you, the software will continue to refine your choices for you.

Focus on fun, we take care of the rest: With Spectrum, you don't have to do anything outside of the application. There is no code to give anyone at the restaurant or store. There is nothing to scan. You do not have to check in. Just enjoy your time

Actionable options: We want you to see promotions you can take advantage of. As a result, you will only see promotions that fall within the range you select.

Your secret is safe with us: Your identity is your business, not ours. We promise to not share any of your personal information. You will not be stuck on a call list or receive spam because of us. We promise.
